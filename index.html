Calgary

Overview

Calgary is a very beautiful and vibrant city in Canada. It is situated in the province of Alberta and includes a lot of history combined with current energy. It is the entrance to Canadian Rockies and it consists of global-scale tourist attraction sites such as Calgary Tower, Banff National Park among others. Calgary is the home to the famous Calgary stampede, but downtown Calgary also has a balanced mixture of nature, city parks, as well as business establishments. The town is also centered with good environment, kind hearted residents and good quality of life between city and countryside life.
